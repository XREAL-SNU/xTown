Hyperspace FX
=================

This asset features 3 scenes filled to the brim with various particle effects.

The idea of this asset was to provide an animated ambience for main menus, but can also be used as in-game effects.

Feel free to contact me if you need help with the asset or if you have problems with particle effects.

=================
Usage
=================

In the demo scenes you should find a Canvas as well as an EventSystem.

These can be deleted to get rid of the scene selection menu in the bottom right.
